# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We are committed to creating a welcoming and respectful community for everyone.

Please:

- Be respectful and considerate.
- Be constructive when giving feedback.
- Be welcoming to new contributors.
- Focus on what is best for the community.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or discrimination of any kind.
- Personal attacks or insulting language.
- Trolling, spamming, or disruptive behavior.
- Any conduct that creates an unwelcoming environment.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, or contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

By participating in this project, you agree to follow these guidelines.
